Three Ingredient Mac and Cheese

A quick and easy way to make mac and cheese from scratch. (Credit J. Kenji Lopez-Alt)

Ingredients

  • 170g macaroni
  • 170g cheddar cheese
  • 170g evaporated milk

Directions

  1. Fill the pan with the macaroni and just enough water to cover it. Add salt and boil on high until almost all the water is evaporated.
  2. Add evaporated milk, stir frequently until the milk starts boiling.
  3. Take the pan off heat, stir in the cheddar cheese until fully combined.
